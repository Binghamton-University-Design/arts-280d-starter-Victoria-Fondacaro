Italian Lemon Braids

This recipe takes the cake-or should I say cookie-for the most fun, delicious, and aesthetic creation I have made.

The cookies are super addicting since they are a shortbread cookie with very subtle lemon flavor any lemon hater will tolerate. They are perfectly sweet with their light lemon glaze on top. Rainbow sprinkles are added since they are featured in several Italian desserts and provide a cute pop of color.

I am very Italian so these cookies were right up my ally, but being so is definetely not required. The original recipe used here is from my friend's family.
