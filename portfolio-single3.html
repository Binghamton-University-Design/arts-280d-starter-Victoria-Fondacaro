Rainbow Eyes

This look originated back in 2018 when I had a phase of creating colorful, intricate eye art. I had even created a social media platform where I showcased my eye art and collaborated with First Aid Beauty, which at the time was a much newer company.

This look in particular is my favorite because of how well different colors from different palettes blended together. Not sure where I'd wear it, but it was fun to create!
