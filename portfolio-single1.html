East Hampton History

These spreads are part of my final project for ARTS 305 - Intro to Graphic Design Software class. We were tasked to make an encyclopedia about anything and I chose the niche town of East Hampton since I grew up there for a while and the quirky town will forever have my heart.

We were challenged to create several entries and use monochrome images which I made blue and grey on Photoshop. Adobe InDesign was used to assemble the spreads. These are my favorite spreads since they use many graphic elements and showcase the town's history in a creative way.

This project made me learn even more about the town I love through research and I was happy to present something meaniful through art. I hope to buy a house in East Hampton again someday!
